In 2022, the ART.STADT gallery in Hamburg hosted an exhibition called LOVE.STADT, a show featuring work exploring the concept of Robot Love and Future Fetish.

Working with early SD1.5 stable diffusion models, Vincent iterated with a prompt featuring an ambiguous slurry of keywords pertaining to `androgenous body, eyes, lips, curves, close up portrait, epic light, melting liquid, acrylic painting, abstract, in glass and black latex fluid, hyperrealistic, 3d sculpture, bokeh, long exposure, art noveau, artistic sketch,  film still, body paint, shallow depth of field, dithering, animation style of Alberto Mielgo` etc. From here, interesting images were cherry picked and exhibited in the show.

In 2026, the Latent Latex piece curates selections from LOVE.STADT, sorts them by high dimensional visual similarity, and creates a smooth latent travel animation, morphing between each image resulting in a smooth, sublime animation
